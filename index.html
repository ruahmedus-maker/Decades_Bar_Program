Standard Operating Procedures

Consistency is key to our success. Follow these procedures to maintain our high standards of service and safety.

Opening Checklist

  1. Arrive 15 minutes before scheduled shift
  2. Check inventory and restock as needed
  3. Set up garnish station with fresh ingredients
  4. Ensure all equipment is clean and functional
  5. Review daily specials and promotions

Closing Procedures

  1. Complete final customer service 15 minutes before closing
  2. Clean and sanitize all workstations
  3. Restock for next shift
  4. Complete cash-out and POS reports
  5. Secure all alcohol and equipment

Red Bull Drinks & Bombs

Vodka Red Bull
Ingredients:
  • 1½ oz Vodka (or any liquor)
  • Half can of Red Bull to fill
Instructions:
  1. Add liquor to big cup with ice
  2. Fill with Red Bull
  3. Serve with straw
Bombs (Jäger, Orange, etc)
Ingredients:
  • 1½ oz Liquor (in shot glass)
  • Half can of Red Bull in Big cup
Instructions:

  2. Pour Red Bull into Big cup

Glassware Guide

Decades Large Branded Glass

Primary Use: High-volume cocktails and mixed drinks

Includes:
  • Water, Juice, and Soda
  • Any Long Island Iced Tea variations
  • Any cocktail with Red Bull
  • Bombs for Red Bull
  • Decades Signature/Seasonal feature cocktails
  • Any "double" cocktail

Note: May be used as a double shooter upon specific customer request

Decades Frosted Glass

Primary Use: Spirit-forward drinks served without large-volume mixers

Includes:
  • Single or double "neat" pours of any liquor
  • Single-pour cocktails (e.g., Vodka & Soda, Rum & Coke)
  • Single or double pours of liquor "on the rocks"
  • Any request for a Neat/Straight Up shot (e.g., Bourbon Neat, Scotch Straight Up)

Decades Shot Glass

Primary Use: All shot servings

Includes:
  • Shots of liquor
  • All shooters

Standard: All shots are to be poured below the rim

⚠️ Important Thursday Policy

Security & Underage Drinking Protocol:

On Thursdays, to assist security in detecting underage drinking, all cocktails that are normally served in the Large Branded Glass must be served in the Frosted Glass instead.

This includes:

  • Long Island Iced Teas
  • Cocktails with Red Bull
  • Double cocktails

Please adhere to this policy for the entire Thursday night operation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about procedures, policies, and best practices.

What should I do if a customer appears intoxicated?

Stop serving alcohol to the customer immediately. Offer water and food instead. If necessary, arrange for a safe ride home. Document the incident in the logbook and notify management.

How do I handle a spill or broken glass?

Immediately cordon off the area to prevent accidents. Use the spill kit located under the bar. For broken glass, use a broom and dustpan, never your hands. Dispose of broken glass in the designated container.

What's the procedure for processing a return?

All returns must be approved by a manager. Process the return through the POS system using the original payment method when possible. Document the reason for the return in the system notes.
